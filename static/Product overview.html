SCALA2

GRUNDFOS SCALA2 is a light, composite, variable speed pump that adjusts performance according to water demand, providing constant pressure to a small to medium sized house. The fully integrated pump includes a motor, tank, sensor, drive and non-return valve in one compact unit that is quick and easy to install.

SCALA2 is self-priming, allowing for use from both above ground water sources and the water-cooled motor ensures quiet operation.
